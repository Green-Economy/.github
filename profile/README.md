### 🌟 Our Mission
An open-source research and engineering labs building telemetry dashboards, smart grids, and RESTful APIs for sustainable energy management and eco-friendly product commerce.

---

### 📂 Core Repositories & Systems

⚡ **Green Energy Dashboard**: React frontend client monitoring solar and wind telemetry.
🛍️ **Green Economy API**: Express & MongoDB backend managing sustainable products.
🔌 **Chat Hub**: Real-time Socket.io and Redis server supporting instant messaging rooms.
